    A bit about me...

    My name is Anaelia Ovalle, or Elia for short, and I am a senior data science student at the University of San Francisco located in the Bay Area. I moved to the United States from the Dominican Republic 4 years ago with the aspiration of becoming a quantitative research scientist. As a deeply curious individual, I love investigating data and working with D3 to explore new information in an equally intuitive and aesthetically pleasing manner.
